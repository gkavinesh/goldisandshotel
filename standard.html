Rooms

Standard Sea View

In standard sea view rooms, guests enjoy a coastal escape with comfort and scenic ocean views. These rooms feature individually controlled air-conditioning, plush king-size or twin beds, and a functional work desk.

Complimentary Wi-Fi and an international dial telephone keep guests connected, while entertainment is provided by a Smart TV. Safety is ensured with a safety box, and guests can enjoy hot beverages with tea and coffee making facilities.

A cozy sitting area offers a space to relax and take in the ocean vistas, while the modern bathroom includes a rain shower, bathrobes, and slippers for added comfort. Connecting room options provide flexibility for group stays.
