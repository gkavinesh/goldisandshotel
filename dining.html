Our Dining

Savor the Flavors of Goldi Sands


Dining at Goldi Sands is a specialty by itself. The hotel is renowned for excellent sea food bought straight from the fishing boats in the vicinity of the hotel. You can choose from a wide list of mouthwatering sea food delicacies prepared by our experienced chefs; the freshness and flavor of the sea food cuisine is certainly going to make you ask for more. You can also tuck into a wide and varied A la Carte menu featuring both international and local cuisine prepared to suite the discerning taste of guests. Still more if there is a special dish you wish to have made, just request it and our chefs will be more than happy to prepare it for you. And what better way to enjoy the culinary delights than a bottle of fine wine from our well stocked bar which provides a wide variety of coolers and cocktails to lager and liquor, sipped over a game of pool.
