Goldi Sands Hotel - Negombo

Discover the Allure of Negombo


Goldi Sands Hotel, situated on the picturesque beaches of Negombo, offers 131 luxurious rooms catering to every guest's needs. With options ranging from deluxe sea-view rooms to spacious suites, each accommodation boasts stylish decor and modern amenities. Guests can bask in the warmth of the sun by the pool, indulge in water sports, or simply unwind on the pristine beach. As night falls, the hotel comes alive with entertainment and delectable dining experiences, including private candlelit dinners by the ocean. Our dedicated staff is committed to providing personalized service, ensuring a memorable and enjoyable stay for all our guests at Goldi Sands Hotel.

EXPERIENCES

Our Deluxe Wing

Goldi Sands Deluxe Rooms offer a luxurious stay with a host of amenities for guest comfort. Each room features air-conditioning, a choice of king-size or twin beds, and a spacious work desk. Complimentary Wi-Fi and international dial telephones keep guests connected, while smart TVs provide entertainment. Safety boxes and mini-bars offer convenience, along with tea/coffee facilities. Guests can relax in the sitting area, enjoy rain showers in the bathroom, and use provided bathrobes, slippers, and hairdryers. Connecting rooms are available for families or groups, ensuring a pleasant stay for all.

EXPERIENCES

Dutch Canal Boat Ride

Take an interesting guided boat trip down the 100 meter long historical Dutch canal. This canal was once used as a supply route by the Dutch administration ruling the country and is today a major tourist attraction and a means to an interesting sightseeing expedition.

Comfortable private boats equipped with safety gear and knowledgeable personnel will take you through traditional fishing villages that go past old churches and other historical buildings right down to the Muthurajawala marshes; an interesting wetland that is home to a unique coastal ecosystem.

DINING

Indulge in Culinary Excellence at Goldi Sands


Our Dining

Goldi Sands offers an exceptional dining experience, renowned for its fresh seafood sourced directly from nearby fishing boats. Guests can savor mouthwatering seafood delicacies prepared by experienced chefs, as well as international and local cuisine from an extensive à la carte menu. The well-stocked bar offers a selection of fine wines and cocktails to complement your meal. Theme night buffets, including Chinese, Italian, and Seafood Extravaganza, add excitement to dining, along with beach barbecues under the stars. For a romantic touch, private dining can be arranged on the beach or by the pool. With attentive service and a cozy atmosphere, dining at Goldi Sands promises excellent value and a memorable experience.
