Our Rooms

Seaside Serenity with Ocean Views


The hotel boasts of One Hundred and Thirty one air-conditioned double rooms, which consists of Five Junior Suites, Two Duplex suites, Fifty Seven Deluxe Sea View Rooms, Fifty Seven Sea View Standard Rooms and Ten Economy Rooms which can accommodate 2 pax each and the rest of the rooms having its own private balcony / terrace offering excellent views of the Indian Ocean and the beautiful landscaped garden, which can accommodate 2 pax plus 2 kids each. All rooms elegantly decorated in keeping with the theme of the hotel are equipped with telephone, piped music, mini refrigerator, electronic safe, hair dryer, tea and coffee making facilities and en-suite bathrooms with hot and cold water. The hotel also provides 24 hour room service, doctor on call, IDD & internet facilities, foreign currency encashment and laundry services. From the seclusion of your room you can watch the many moods of the ocean from total calmness to high tide when the waves crash on the shore with intensity. You can also observe the fishing boats as they depart for the nightly journey amidst the golden glow of the setting sun.
